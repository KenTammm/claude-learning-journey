## Template 1: LinkedIn Post — Resort Owners

**AI Prompt:**
> Write a LinkedIn post targeting resort owners in Australia who are considering adding glamping accommodation to their property.
>
> Audience profile: They already run accommodation businesses, care most about revenue uplift and ROI, and their biggest fear is high maintenance costs and no market demand.
>
> Product: We supply luxury glamping tent solutions — fully designed living spaces including flooring, bathrooms, and interior layouts. We provide the design system, material estimation, and guide clients to work with local builders.
>
> Format: LinkedIn post, 150–200 words, professional but conversational tone, end with a soft call-to-action (not a hard sell). Include one specific proof point about revenue potential.

**Example Output:**
---
Most resort owners we speak to aren't looking to tear up their property and start again.

They're looking for the next thing — something that attracts a different guest, commands a higher nightly rate, and doesn't require a full construction project to get off the ground.

That's exactly what a well-designed glamping addition can do.

Our clients typically see 30–50% higher nightly rates for glamping accommodation versus standard rooms. And because we supply the full design system — flooring, bathrooms, interior layout — your local builder handles the construction. No permanent structures. No major council hurdles.

If you've got land and you're thinking about what's next, it might be worth a conversation.

What's your current biggest challenge when it comes to expanding accommodation? Drop it in the comments — happy to share what we've seen work.

## Template 2: LinkedIn Post — Farm & Land Owners

**AI Prompt:**
> Write a LinkedIn post targeting Australian farm owners or rural landowners who want to create a new income stream from their land using glamping.
>
> Audience profile: They own scenic or rural land but have limited hospitality experience. They care most about creating passive income, and their biggest fears are not knowing where to start and not having enough visitors.
>
> Product: We supply luxury glamping tent solutions with full design, material estimation, and builder guidance. Clients don't need to know construction — we guide them through the whole process.
>
> Format: LinkedIn post, 150–200 words, warm and approachable tone, speak to the "empty land = missed opportunity" angle, end with a question to encourage engagement.

**Example Output:**
---
There's a moment a lot of landowners have.

You're looking out over your property — the trees, the view, the quiet — and you think: *someone would pay to be here.*

They would. And more than you might expect.

Glamping has quietly become one of the fastest-growing segments in Australian tourism. Travellers are actively searching for unique, natural experiences — and farm stays and rural properties are exactly what they're looking for.

The barrier isn't the land. It's knowing where to start.

That's what we help with. We handle the design, estimate your materials, and guide you through working with a local builder. You don't need construction experience. You need good land — and a plan.

Many of our clients started with 3–4 tents and grew from there once they saw the demand.

Do you own rural land you've been thinking about activating? What's held you back so far?

**AI Prompt:**
> Write a LinkedIn post targeting land developers or tourism investors in Australia who are evaluating glamping as a commercial project.
>
> Audience profile: They think in terms of investment returns and scalability. They care about total project cost vs revenue potential, and their biggest fears are cost blowouts and legal compliance issues.

>
> Format: LinkedIn post, 150–200 words, confident and data-forward tone, speak to the investment case, end with a call-to-action to connect directly.

**Example Output:**
---
The glamping market in Australia is growing — and the investment case is straightforward.

Lower build cost than permanent accommodation. Higher nightly rates than standard rooms. Faster time to income. And a growing pool of travellers actively choosing unique experiences over traditional hotels.

We've supplied glamping projects from 4 tents to 40+. The design system is scalable, the material estimation process is precise, and we support compliance guidance across Australian councils and jurisdictions.

What that means for you: you know your costs before you commit, and you're not navigating the regulatory side alone.

If you're evaluating a tourism project and want to understand the numbers — land size requirements, cost per tent, projected payback periods — I'm happy to walk you through what we've seen across comparable projects.

DM me or drop a comment. Always open to a direct conversation.

**Subject:** Adding a glamping option to [Resort Name] — worth 15 minutes?

If this is something you've been considering, I'd love to share what's worked for properties similar to yours — even just a 15-minute call to see if it makes sense.

Would that work for you?

## Discernment Checklist

Before using any AI-generated copy, check:

- [ ] Does it speak to the client's ROI, not just our product features?
- [ ] Is the tone right for the audience? (Resort owners = professional, Farm owners = warm, Developers = data-forward)
- [ ] Are any specific claims (percentages, timelines) verified and accurate?
- [ ] Does it sound like us — or could it be for any glamping company?
- [ ] Would you be comfortable sending this to a real client today?

If any answer is NO — revise before sending.
