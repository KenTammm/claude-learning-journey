# AI-Assisted Marketing Playbook
## Project Plan & Delegation Analysis

> **Author:** Ken Tam | Marketing Officer, Sydney  
> **Context:** Course project for AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations (CCA-F Learning Journey)  
> **Company:** Luxury Glamping Tent Solutions Provider (B2B)  
> **Last updated:** April 2026

---

## Project Vision

> A simple, practical AI guide that helps the Marketing and Sales team use AI to write B2B glamping copy, follow up with clients, and produce reports — easy enough for anyone to pick up immediately, even without AI experience.

**Who it's for:** Marketing team (2 people) + Sales team  
**What it solves:**
- Help team write better B2B copy faster
- Save time on reports and proposals
- Give everyone a shared standard for using AI at work

---

## Company Context

**What we do:** Supply luxury glamping tent solutions to B2B clients — resort owners, land developers, and tourism operators who want to build unique accommodation experiences.

**Our product is not just a tent. It includes:**
- Full interior design (flooring, bathroom, layout)
- Built for comfort in natural environments
- Complete design system + material estimation
- Guidance to work with local builders

**Our role:** Design + solution provider. We help businesses turn empty land into profitable glamping accommodation.

**Why this matters for AI:** Our product is complex and visual. Good copy needs to speak to business ROI, not just aesthetics. AI needs strong context about our product to be useful.

---

## Playbook Structure (5 Parts)

### Part 1 — Define Audience & Goals
A one-page reference explaining who our B2B clients are and what they care about, so AI always has the right context.

### Part 2 — B2B Copywriting Templates
AI-assisted copy for key channels: website, LinkedIn, email campaigns — written for resort owners, farm operators, and land developers.

### Part 3 — Sales Follow-Up Email Templates
Ready-to-use AI prompts that help Sales write personalised follow-up emails for different client scenarios.

### Part 4 — Report Production Workflow
Step-by-step guide for using AI to speed up monthly reports, campaign summaries, and client proposals.

### Part 5 — AI Usage Guidelines
Company standards for using AI: what's allowed, what's not, and how to keep our brand voice consistent.

## Key Delegation Insights

**1. Context is everything**  
Our product is complex — AI needs detailed context about luxury glamping, B2B clients, and our design-led approach before it can produce useful copy. Without this, output will be generic.

**2. Sales team needs simplicity**  
The Playbook must work for people with zero AI experience. Templates and copy-paste prompts will be more useful than explanations.

**3. Human judgment protects brand voice**  
AI can draft, but the final call on tone and messaging must stay human. Luxury B2B copy has a specific register that's easy to get wrong.

**4. This is Augmentation, not Automation**  
We're not replacing marketing work — we're making it faster and better by combining human expertise with AI speed.
