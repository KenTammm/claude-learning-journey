# Use Case 1: Instagram Campaign Performance Analysis

## Background
Marketing Officer at a Sydney-based K-pop inspired men's fashion brand,
targeting Australian males aged 24+

## Prompt Used
"I'm a Marketing Officer at a Sydney-based men's fashion brand with a 
K-pop inspired aesthetic. Here is last month's Instagram data: 10 posts, 
total reach 15,000, total engagement 800, highest performing post: autumn 
layering outfit, lowest performing post: product-only flat lay, average 
Stories views 200. Please analyze the performance, extract key insights, 
and recommend improvements for next month targeting Australian males aged 24+"

## Key Insights from Claude
- Engagement rate 5.3% — above industry average of 1-3%
- Lifestyle content outperforms flat lay product shots
- Stories severely underperforming (1.3% vs healthy 10-20%)

## Claude's Recommendations
- Minimum 6 lifestyle/outfit posts per month
- Post Stories 3-5x per week with interactive elements
- Collaborate with Sydney/Melbourne micro-influencers (5k-50k followers)

## Business Value
Reduced campaign analysis time from ~2 hours to 5 minutes.
Generated benchmark comparisons and actionable targets automatically.
